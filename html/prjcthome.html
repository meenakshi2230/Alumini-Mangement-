About us

Pydah College of Engineering is yet another jewel in the Crown of Pydah Educational Group and was established in 2009 in Kakinada, East Godavari district,south of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is one of top Engineering colleges in East Godavari District of Andhrapradesh.. adhering to its core values,the instuite gives top priority to ethical values amd high standards and a commitement to value based education.pydah college is known for its EAMCET entrance test where over 250,000 students take the test every year for around 600 seats in its undergraduate engineering courses.All the selected top candidates for engineering programs enroll at the College of Engineering patavala ,Kakinada.

Admission into BTech is based on EAMCET or ECET ranks;admission into MTech is based on Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering or PGECET scores; and admission into MCA is based on ICET rank. Students in the state with the highest grades in the above entrance examinations get admission to the college.
Admission into its undergraduate engineering courses is extremely competitive with less than 1% of the students from the Open Category getting selected to study at our College of Engineering Kakinada, pydah college through EAMCET entrance examination every year.
